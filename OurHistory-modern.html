Our History

Twins Security Services was founded in 1993 with a vision to provide superior security services to the greater Los Angeles area. What began as a small operation with just a handful of security officers has grown into one of the most trusted security service providers in the region.

Our Journey

1993 - Foundation

Twins Security Services was established to meet the growing demand for professional security services in Los Angeles.

1998 - Expansion

After five successful years, we expanded our services to include specialized security solutions for various industries, including commercial, residential, and industrial sectors.

2003 - Certification & Training

We established partnerships with leading security training academies to ensure our officers receive the best training in the industry.

2008 - Technology Integration

We began integrating advanced security technologies with our traditional security services to provide comprehensive protection solutions.

2013 - 20th Anniversary

We celebrated 20 years of service excellence and expanded our client base to include major corporations and high-profile venues.

Today

With over 28 years of experience, Twins Security Services continues to be a leader in the security industry, providing customized security solutions to meet the unique needs of each client.

Throughout our history, we have remained committed to our founding principles of integrity, professionalism, and exceptional service. We have built our reputation on providing reliable, high-quality security services that our clients can count on.

As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to evolving with the changing security landscape, adopting new technologies and methodologies to ensure we continue to provide the best possible protection for our clients.
