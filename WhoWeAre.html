STAFF

Twins Security Services considers our staff to be our first line of customers, and are very important to us. We do this to ensure they are happy with us, they are here longer, resulting in a slow staff turnover and guaranteeing that you are matched with a security guard who is consistent in his or her service.

RECRUITMENT

Providing quality service starts with attracting the best people possible. It is for that reason, that we offer competitive wages in the industry.

COMPLIANCE

Twins Security Services is committed to being the employer of choice in the Los Angeles area and the success of the services we provide rests immeasurably in the quality of our staff. For this reason, our comprehensive approach to hiring the best qualified individuals include:

  1. Drug Testing - Twins Security Services is committed to a drug free environment. As part of this commitment every new hire is subject to pre-employment drug test.
  2. Background check - We trust an industry leader third party to help ensure the trustworthiness of our staff.
  3. Interviewing - Every candidate is interviewed personally to evaluate their character and personality to ensure he or she is the best fit for our company.
  4. Orientation - Every security service officer must complete successfully a period of orientation and best practices prior to deployment in the field.
